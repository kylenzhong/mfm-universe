# Gemini Video Generation Prompt — MFM Universe Intro

---

## PRIMARY PROMPT (use this in Veo / Gemini video generation)

```
An animated intro sequence in the exact visual style of the Silicon Valley HBO show
opening credits. Isometric 3D low-poly city viewed from a 45-degree elevated aerial
angle. Warm afternoon golden-hour lighting with long shadows cast across the city.
Clean, crisp, colorful flat-shaded 3D architecture with sharp edges and minimal
surface detail.

The camera begins with a wide aerial establishing shot showing a dense Silicon
Valley-style tech campus city block from above. It slowly pans and rotates clockwise
across the cityscape, gliding smoothly at rooftop level.

In the dead center of the city, enormous 3D extruded block letters spell "MFM
UNIVERSE" in bold red, casting long shadows onto the buildings and roads below —
exactly like "SILICON VALLEY" appears in the HBO intro. The letters are massive,
slightly tilted, and dominate the frame.

Surrounding the central letters are 10 distinct corporate headquarters buildings,
each with its company logo and name clearly visible on the building facade:

1. HUBSPOT — tall glass office tower, orange accent panels, HubSpot sprocket logo
   in orange on the main facade, "HubSpot" signage on roof.

2. THE HUSTLE — medium-height modern building with orange/red branding, bold "H"
   lightning bolt logo, "The Hustle" lettering across the front.

3. 1-800-GOT-JUNK — industrial warehouse-style building with blue and orange panels,
   large truck illustration on the side, "1-800-GOT-JUNK?" in big signage.

4. MORNING BREW — trendy mid-rise office with warm brown facade, large coffee mug
   sign on the roof, "Morning Brew" neon-style sign glowing on front.

5. RXBAR — sleek low-rise building with bold red exterior, "RXBAR" stenciled in
   giant white letters across the entire front face.

6. BEBO — purple-themed tech campus building with speech bubble logo on the side,
   three dot pattern, "Bebo" in large purple neon.

7. THE MILK ROAD — modern loft-style building with teal and white exterior, milk
   carton shape incorporated into the architecture, "Milk Road" in white on facade.

8. HAMPTON — dark luxury high-rise with gold trim, shield emblem above the entrance,
   "Hampton" in gold serif lettering.

9. BEEHIIV — contemporary glass building with green honeycomb pattern on exterior
   cladding, hexagonal logo prominent, "beehiiv" in green.

10. MARQUIS JET — sleek low-profile building with aviation aesthetic, jet silhouette
    on the side, navy blue and silver color scheme.

Between the buildings: wide roads with tiny cars driving, green trees and shrubs,
small people walking on sidewalks, a helicopter hovering near one rooftop, rooftop
patios with tiny tables. Subtle animated details: car headlights blinking, windows
lighting up at dusk, a small airplane contrail in the sky.

The scene has depth and parallax — closer buildings are larger and more detailed,
distant buildings fade with atmospheric haze. Roads converge to a vanishing point.

Style references: Silicon Valley HBO intro, SimCity isometric, low-poly game
aesthetic, clean corporate animation. Color palette: warm sky gradient from deep
purple-blue at top to golden orange at horizon, earthy tan ground, company brand
colors on each building.

The animation ends with a slow zoom-out revealing the full "MFM UNIVERSE" city,
then a slow fade to black.

Duration: 20-30 seconds. Frame rate: 24fps. No dialogue. No music described.
```
