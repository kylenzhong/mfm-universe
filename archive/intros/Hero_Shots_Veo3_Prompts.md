## GENERATION STRATEGY

### Order of Generation (prioritize API credits):
1. **Shot C** (`SB04_parachute_wide.png`) — This is the most complex story beat and the most visually striking. Test this first.
2. **Shot A** (`SB01_balloons_approaching.png`) — Balloon approach, simpler motion.
3. **Shot D or E** — Wide establishing, pick whichever angle you prefer.
4. **Shot B** — Hustle descending on Hampton.
5. Remaining shots as B-roll.

### Settings:
- **Duration:** Start with 4 seconds to test, then try 8 seconds for the best ones
- **Aspect ratio:** 16:9
- **If Veo 3 supports style reference images:** Upload an HBO Silicon Valley opening credits screenshot alongside your render

### Post-Production Plan:
- Company sign text WILL get garbled by the AI — composite clean text overlays in post (After Effects, CapCut, or Canva)
- The Cal AI parachute text is most critical to preserve — if it's unreadable in the generation, composite "Cal AI" text on the green canopy
- Color-correct all clips to match the same warm golden palette before editing together
- Suggested sequence: Wide establishing → Balloon approach → Balloon collision → Hustle descends → Hampton grows → Cut to parachute wide → Cal AI lands → MyFitnessPal grows → Final wide shot
